## üöÄ Publishing Strategy

### Option A: Sequential Publishing (Recommended)
Publish one article every 2-3 days to build momentum:
- **Day 1**: Publish Part 1
- **Day 3**: Publish Part 2, update Part 1 with link to Part 2
- **Day 5**: Publish Part 3, update previous parts
- Continue pattern...

**Advantages**:
- Builds anticipation and engagement
- Allows you to respond to feedback and adjust later parts
- Better for SEO (steady content flow)
- Gives time to promote each article

### Option B: Batch Publishing
Publish all 6 articles at once, then cross-link:
- Publish all articles as drafts
- Get all Medium URLs
- Update all placeholder links
- Publish simultaneously

**Advantages**:
- Complete series available immediately
- All internal links work from day 1
- Good for readers who want to binge-read

## üí° Tips for Maximum Impact

1. **Timing**: Publish Tuesday-Thursday mornings (EST) for best visibility
2. **Length**: Your articles are well-sized (8-15 min reads are ideal)
3. **First 100 words**: Make them count - hook readers immediately
4. **Code blocks**: Keep them concise and well-commented
5. **Subheadings**: Use them generously for scannability
6. **Bold/Italic**: Emphasize key points but don't overuse
7. **Lists**: Readers love them (you're reading one now!)
